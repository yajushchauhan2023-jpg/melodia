About us

We make music learning feel supported, structured, and joyful.

Melodia replaces scattered tutorials and expensive one-off lessons with affordable AI guidance, live teachers, and steady progress tracking.

Platform foundation

Built like a launch-ready SaaS product.

Melodia is designed around a Next.js frontend, Node.js API layer, PostgreSQL records, Clerk auth, Stripe billing, Cloudinary storage, WebSockets, TensorFlow audio models, OCR music parsing, LLM tutoring memory, Calendar scheduling, and LiveKit video.
